About Us

Tsheryl's BnB


Welcome to Tsheryl's BnB, your home away from home! Nestled in the serene neighborhood of Kwartkop, Centurion, Pretoria, Tsheryl's BnB offers a perfect blend of comfort and convenience. Located at House No. 97 Dadelboom Street, Ext 04, our charming bed and breakfast is an ideal choice for travelers seeking a cozy and peaceful retreat. At Tsheryl's BnB, we pride ourselves on providing exceptional hospitality and personalized service. Each of our well-appointed rooms is designed to ensure a restful stay, featuring comfortable beds, modern amenities, and tasteful decor. Whether you're visiting for business or leisure, our welcoming atmosphere will make you feel right at home. Guests can enjoy a delicious breakfast each morning, made with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. Our communal areas are perfect for relaxing, socializing, or catching up on work. With free Wi-Fi, secure parking, and easy access to local attractions, Tsheryl's BnB caters to all your needs. Conveniently located in Centurion, our BnB is close to shopping centers, restaurants, and entertainment options. Explore the vibrant city of Pretoria or unwind in the tranquil surroundings of Kwartkop. Whatever your plans, Tsheryl's BnB is your perfect base for a memorable stay. Experience the warmth and comfort of Tsheryl's BnB—where every guest is treated like family. Book your stay with us today and discover the best of Centurion hospitality.
