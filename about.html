About The House

Welcome to your dream home! Nestled in a very safe and tranquil neighborhood, this stunning property offers both comfort and luxury. The main house is a spacious two-story, 5-bedroom haven designed for family living and entertaining. On the ground floor, you'll find three inviting bedrooms, two of which share a well-appointed bathroom, while the third features a private en-suite bathroom. The top floor boasts two additional bedrooms that share a bathroom, along with an extra toilet for added convenience. The heart of the home is its open-plan living area, where natural light floods in from multiple angles, creating a warm and welcoming atmosphere. The living room flows seamlessly into a charming dining area adorned with vintage furnishings, perfect for family meals and gatherings. The open kitchen is a chef’s delight, offering ample movement space and modern amenities to make cooking a pleasure. Step out onto the top floor balcony to enjoy fresh air and catch the mesmerizing sunrises and sunsets that Pretoria is known for. In addition to the main house, there is a separate double room outside with its own kitchen and bathroom, ideal for guests or extended family. Parking is a breeze with a two-car garage and additional parking spaces available. This property is not just a house; it’s a place to call home. Come and experience the perfect blend of comfort, style, and convenience. Your new home away from home awaits!.
